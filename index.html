What is it?

DevOps UMN is a one day conference which will feature a mixture of talks, panels, and open space discussions. It will likely be held somewhere on campus during the week of spring break. The exact date and location are to be determined.

Conference Themes

  • Promote awareness of services offered by others, reduce duplication of effort
  • Discover what other groups are doing
  • Improve communication amongst IT groups at the U
  • Discuss DevOps practices and tools

Talk Submission

Have an idea for a talk? Great! To minimize any potential biases, the committee has opted to review submissions in as anonymous fashion as possible. Chris Dinger (ding0057) has agreed to accept the submissions on our behalf. Please send the title and abstract of your talk to him. The deadline for submissions is January 17, 2017.
